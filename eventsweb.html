TSA Regionals

TSA Regionals is the smallest event Westfield participates in. It includes schools in the Northern Virginia Area, and offers a variety of events. Students can be introduced to TSA competitions in a fun and engaging way

TSA Technosphere

The Virginia TSA Technosphere is the state's annual conference where students from across Virginia compete in various STEM-related events, showcase their skills, and engage in leadership development activities. This high-energy event brings together future engineers, designers, and innovators to participate in hands-on challenges, from robotics and coding to public speaking and project presentations. Technosphere provides students with opportunities to network, collaborate, and apply their knowledge

TSA Nationals

TSA Nationals is the largest conference TSA had to offer. You meet with members from almost every state, and get to test yourself at the highest level. This occurs during the summer, and is one of the most fun competitions of any club.
