About TSA Club

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization of students engaged in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). Our school’s TSA chapter provides a platform for students to explore these fields through hands-on activities, leadership development, and competitions at the regional, state, and national levels.

As a TSA member, you’ll have the opportunity to design innovative projects, collaborate with peers, and develop skills that will prepare you for a future in technology and engineering. Whether you're interested in coding, robotics, or graphic design, there’s something for everyone in TSA.
